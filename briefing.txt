General directions
1. For 2D games, you should search for tiles, tilesets, sprites, and sprite sheets.
2. Your project must be written in accordance with the Norm.
3. Your functions should not quit unexpectedly (segfault, bus error, double free, etc.)
4. All heap allocated memory space must be properly freed when necessary. No leaks.
5. Makefile must not relink and must be compiled using the flags -Wall, -Wextra and -Werror
6. Your Makefile must at least contain the rules $(NAME), all, clean, fclean and re.
7. Bonus part must be separated from mandatory part. It must have a bonus rule in makefile.

Introductory directions
1. The argument will be a map in format *.ber
2. You are allowed to use the following external functions
	- open, close, read, write, printf, malloc, free, perror, strerror, exit
	- All functions of the MinilibX
	- Libft
